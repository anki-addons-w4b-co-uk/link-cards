When you add cid:1234567890123 (card id) or nid:1234567890123 (note id) to a card or template this add-ons transforms the text to a clickable hyperlink. When you click it a preview of the card will open in an extra window or the note will be shown in an extra editor window. The latter is experimental, might damage your note and needs to be enabled from the add-on options. For details about cids/nids see e.g. .
